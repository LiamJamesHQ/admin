If we're building on site

Most builds are done in one visit

The majority of the time our team arrives with everything needed and completes the build in one go.

Before then, we will be in touch to check that everything is workable at your property, including access for the team and materials.

Occasionally, depending on your location within our installation route, it works better to do things a little differently. The team may:

  • Arrive with all materials ready to begin immediately
  • Have materials delivered beforehand
  • Begin groundwork or decking before the main structure is assembled

Where materials do arrive a day or two ahead, it allows our carpentry team to maximise their time on site rather than travelling back and forth to Wales.

Installation windows

Why we give windows rather than exact dates

Many customers ask why we provide installation windows rather than exact dates, and it is a fair question.

It comes down to the fact that every installation is a little different.

For example, if the project immediately before yours requires additional groundwork, decking or unexpected site work, our team cannot simply leave that customer halfway through to arrive on another job.

The same courtesy would be extended to you should your installation require additional time.

Giving you a realistic window means we can finish every project to the standard it deserves, including yours.

Mobile sauna customers

DVSA testing before handover

Your trailer must successfully complete DVSA testing before handover.

Testing dates are booked in advance and often give us the first reliable indication of your estimated completion date.

Once your test date is confirmed, we'll be able to provide a much more accurate delivery window.

Frequently asked questions

Answers to what most people ask

Can I make changes to my order?

Small changes may be possible before production begins. Once materials have been ordered or manufacturing has started, changes may not be possible or may incur additional costs.

When will I hear from you?

Typically around the 6 week mark, unless we need to contact you sooner.

Can I get weekly updates?

As every project is bespoke, we generally contact customers at key milestones rather than sending routine updates.

Can my installation date change?

Occasionally, yes. Factors such as weather, previous installations or supplier lead times can affect scheduling. We'll always keep you informed of any significant changes.

Can I add additional items to my order?

Yes. We build everything that complements saunas, in a similar style and using the same materials so it all matches aesthetically. If you are considering anything further, it is worth doing so as early as possible. That way we can aim to bring everything at once and save you on delivery costs.
